Boston Kreme

Rating: ★★★☆☆


The Boston Kreme donut offers a delightful and indulgent experience for chocolate lovers. Topped with a rich chocolate glaze, this donut boasts a soft and squishy texture that is a delight to bite into. The chocolate flavor is apparent from the first bite, providing a satisfying balance of sweetness and cocoa. While the inconsistent size might be a drawback, the evenly cooked nature of the donut ensures a consistent taste and texture. It is worth noting that the Boston Kreme donut stands out among its counterparts as the least messy option, making it a convenient treat to enjoy on the go.

Despite its peculiar description, the Boston Kreme donut brings a unique charm to the Dunkin Donuts menu. With its unmistakable aroma of a freshly baked bread, it evokes a sense of comfort and nostalgia. The donut's decadent chocolate coma and sugary sweetness may be a bit overwhelming for some, but it undeniably satisfies those craving an intense chocolate fix. While it shares similarities with the Bavarian Kreme, the Boston Kreme manages to differentiate itself and impress with its chocolate-forward profile. Overall, the Boston Kreme donut offers a memorable experience for chocolate enthusiasts, combining a soft texture, prominent chocolate flavor, and convenient mess-free enjoyment.




Bavarian Kreme

Rating: ★★☆☆☆


After tasting the Bavarian Kreme donut, I must admit that it left a lot to be desired. The donut resembled the Lemon donut in appearance, with a consistent size, but unfortunately, that was where the similarities ended. The sugar levels in the Bavarian Kreme were off the charts, making it excessively sweet and overpowering. The dandruff-like flaky sugar on top gave it a rather unappealing texture.

Upon taking a bite, the donut had a distinct smell of bread dough mixed with sugar, which wasn't particularly inviting. The weak custard filling inside was disappointing, lacking in flavor and consistency. It seemed to be inconsistently spread within the donut, resulting in some bites having hardly any custard at all. Overall, the Bavarian Kreme donut fell short, with its bland taste and lackluster custard, making it a sad excuse for a donut. The only redeeming factor was the sweetness from the sugar coating, but that alone wasn't enough to salvage this lackluster treat.




Lemon

Rating: ★★★★☆


The Lemon donut is a delightful treat that is coated in a generous layer of powdered sugar, creating an enticing visual appeal. As you take your first bite, you'll experience a snowstorm of sweetness that truly makes this donut a sugar lover's dream. While the shape may be slightly inconsistent, the soft bread is evenly cooked, offering a pleasant texture that complements the overall experience.

One aspect that could have been improved is the lemon scent, as it was not as pronounced as anticipated. However, once you indulge in the donut, you'll be pleasantly surprised by the burst of tangy lemony liquid that awaits inside. This delectable filling has a similar texture to maple syrup, adding a delightful contrast to the soft bread. Although the sugar taste might be overwhelming for some, it perfectly balances the tanginess of the lemon, creating a harmonious combination that keeps you coming back for more.

The Lemon donut truly shines. The avalanche of powdered sugar sets it apart visually, and the even cooking ensures a consistently enjoyable bite. While the scent may be underwhelming, the burst of lemony liquid within more than makes up for it. If you're a fan of tangy-sweet flavors and don't mind a sugary adventure, this Lemon donut is the perfect choice to satisfy your cravings.
